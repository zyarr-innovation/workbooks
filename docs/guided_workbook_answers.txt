Modifying a Single Group
Ex. 1: Sum of x and y, decreased by 10
1. Translate the "package" (sum of x and y) → (__x__ + __y__)
2. "Decreased by" means subtract 10 from that package.
(__x__ + __y__) − __10__ ← Answer
_________________________________________________________________
Ex. 2: 10 decreased by the sum of x and z
1. Start with the first number → __10__
2. Translate the "package" to be subtracted (sum of x and z) → (__x__ + __z__)
__10__ − (__x__ + __z__) ← Answer
Self-Check: Why use parentheses? Because you are subtracting the whole sum, not just the x.
_________________________________________________________________
Working with Two Groups
Ex. 3: Sum of x and z, decreased by the sum of a and b
1. Translate the first "package" → (__x__ + __z__)
2. Translate the second "package" → (__a__ + __b__)
3. Use subtraction to connect them.
(__x__ + __z__) − (__a__ + __b__) ← Answer
_________________________________________________________________
Ex. 4: Difference of x and y less the sum of z and p
1. Translate the first "package" (difference means subtraction) → (__x__ − __y__)
2. Translate the second "package" (sum means addition) → (__z__ + __p__)
3. Use "less" (subtraction) to connect them.
(__x__ − __y__) − (__z__ + __p__) ← Answer
